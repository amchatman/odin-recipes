New Orleans Gumbo

Gumbo is a popular stew in Louisanana and is the offical state cruisine. The soup is made of meat,shellfish,celery,onions,okra,and bell peppers.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of all purpose flour
  • 2 small young chickens
  • 1 cup yellow onion
  • 1 small onion for chicken broth
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 cup celery chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
  • Kosher salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 6 ounces fresh okra
  • 3/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 pounds andouille sausage sliced
  • 1 1/2 raw shrimp peeled and deveined
  • 8 scallions sliced
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp gumbo filet
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 ounce dried shrimp

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Dutch oven or a heavy pot and add 3/4 cup of oil then add 1 cup of flour gradually and stir continuously (with a wooden spoon you must continue to stir from start to finish) the heat should be on med-low and add in flour until the rue is a dark chocolate color and a thick consistently.
  2. Add pre-cut bell peppers, onions and celery to the rue and stir until the onions are translucent. Gradually Add 6 cups of your chicken broth to the mix.
  3. Add the seasonings
  4. aut√© sausages, chicken and okra and dried shrimp
  5. Add everything to the pot and give it a good stir.
  6. Let the pot come to a slow rumble and cover for about 30 minutes
  7. After 30 minutes add the shrimp
  8. Add 1 tsp gumbo filet to pot.
  9. Turn on low and simmer for at least one hour.
